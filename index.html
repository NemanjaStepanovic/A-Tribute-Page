Nick Vujičić

Motivational speaker, Christian evangelist

Nicholas James "Nick" Vujičić born December 4 1982 is an Australian (Serbian origin) Christian evangelist and motivational speaker born with Phocomelia, a rare disorder characterised by the absence of legs and arms. As a child, he struggled mentally and emotionally as well as physically, but eventually came to terms with his disability. He presents motivational speeches worldwide which focus on living with disabilities and finding hope and meaning in life.

Nick Vujičić's short biography in years:

  • December 4 1982: Born in Melbourne, Australia
  • 1990: Won the Australian Young Citizen Award
  • 2001: First speaking engagement at age 19
  • 2003: Graduated from Griffith University at the age of 21 with a Bachelor of Commerce degree, with a double major in accountancy and financial planning
  • 2005: Nominated for the young Australian of the Year
  • 2007: Vujičić was a recipient of a Well Done Award through the Kingdom Assignment organization; Vujičić founded Attitude is Altitude, a secular motivational speaking company
  • March 2008: Vujičić was interviewed by Bob Cummings for the 20/20 American television show
  • 2010: Life Without Limits: Inspiration of a Ridiculously Good Life
  • 2012: Your Life Without Limits; married Kanae Miyahara
  • 2013: Limitless: Devotions for a Ridiculously Good Life; Unstoppable: The Incredible Power of Faith in Action; first son, Kiyoshi James, was born
  • 2014: The Power of Unstoppable Faith
  • 2015: Stand Strong; second son, Dejan Levi, was born
  • 2016: Love Without Limits
